The Silent Orchard

by Marina Coates · Fiction · 2023

A quiet, aching novel about three sisters who return to their family orchard the summer their mother disappears, and the secrets buried beneath the oldest trees.

"The Silent Orchard" unfolds over a single summer, told in alternating chapters between the three Whitmore sisters as they confront old rivalries and a mystery that has haunted their family for a decade. Coates writes with a spare, lyrical style that lingers long after the final page.
